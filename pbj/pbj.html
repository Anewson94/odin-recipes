Peanutbutter & Jelly Recipe

Peanutbutter & Jelly is a childhood staple for many. Its extreme ease and great taste make it one of the best snacks you can make. We'll start off with what you need to make it, as well as the steps.

What you will need:

  • Your favorite peanutbutter
  • Your favorite flavor of jelly
  • The freshes bread you can get
  • Optional
    • Fruit
    • Chocolate

Now what youve been waiting for... THE RECIPE!

  1. Lay two slices of bread flat.
  2. Get prefered peanut butter and jelly
  3. Spread thouroughly on both slices. (amount varries on preference)
  4. Place both slices together
  5. Enjoy!
