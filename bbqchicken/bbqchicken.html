Barbeque Chicken Recipe

Its hard to go wrong with a chicken dinner. Sometimes plain baked chicken isnt enough though. If you're like me, you like to shake things up sometimes. What better way than some delicious Barbeque Chicken. Here's what you will need.

  • Boneless Chicken Breast
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Your Preffered Seasoning
  • 1/2 Cup of Your Favorite BBQ Sauce

Now what you've been waiting for, the recipe. Full recipe Here but a brief explanation will be below.

  1. Prepare grill to cook at 450 degrees farenheit
  2. Drizzle chicken with olive oil
  3. Place chicken in oven 5-6 minutes then flip
  4. Cook for 4-5 minutes then coat with BBQ sauce
  5. Cook till internal temperature reaches 160 degrees
  6. Enjoy!
