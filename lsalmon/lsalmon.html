Lemon Pepper Salmon Recipe

Salmon, possibly the best fish is my go to any time I dont know what to cook. It looks great, smells great, and tastes even better. So good it makes you want to "slap yo mama". This page will be dedicated to making Lemon Pepper Salmon.

What will you need?

  • Salmon
  • Lemon
  • Black Pepper
  • Herbs
  • Olive Olive

Full recipe steps provided Here but we'll provide a brief explanation on this page.

  1. Cool salmon to room temperature
  2. Place salmon on baking pan
  3. Lay salmon over herbs, cover salmon in seasoning and lemon slices
  4. Bake for 15-20 minutes
  5. Enjoy!
